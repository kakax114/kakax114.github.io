Finalists Exhibition and Awards Ceremony Announcement

The 2014 NTD Television International Figure Painting Competition will hold the finalist exhibition from Dec. 1 – 6th, 2014. Exhibition location, award ceremony, and related activities is as follows:
Exhibition Address:
Non Profit Resources, LTD.
4 West 43rd Street
New York, NY 10036
(Between 5th & 6th Avenue)

The Organizing Committee will hold the following events to help participants broaden their creative thinking, and foster artistic exchange.
